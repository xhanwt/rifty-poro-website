Some commands have alias. So, don't worry. Try typing in short form as you like and see if it works. You can also use slah commands. ðŸ˜›
Asterisk marked are optional. If the champ name is more than one word, put '-' instead of spaces or do not add spaces at all. eg., For lee sin, just type lee-sin or leesin.
r!ability [ability number] - Specific champion's specific ability.
Ability numbers: passive or 0, 1, 2, 3,...

r!build [champ] - Top item build of a specific champion.

r!champ [champ] - A specific champion's stats.

r!cmd - All avilable commands.

r!concluded - The results of recently concluded league matches.

r!follow - Follow current channel of the guild to LOL:WildRift's official twitter.

r!help - Help info.

r!poro - Poro's info.

r!prefix [new]* - Setting for prefix.

r!profile - Your saved in-game profile.

r!save - Save your in-game profile.

r!so [champ] - Skill order of a specific champion.

r!sr [champ] - Spells and runes of a specific champion.

r!stats [gamemode] [region] [tier]* - Champion leaderboard and stats.

r!unfollow - Unfollow to LOL:WildRift's official twitter.

r!upcoming - Ongoing and upcoming league matches and watch links.
